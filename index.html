- THE BAND -

We love music

Evolution are recognised as one of Ireland's most exciting bands on the wedding & corporate circuit. 'With their unique set, energy & style, these guys know how to entertain. This 4 piece, all singing male rock band have evolved over the years, to become all that is required of a successful party band. So from rock n roll, jives & pop... to waltzes, country & rock ...'These guys will adapt, to keep your dancefloor packed'

So who have the Evolution lads shared a stage with .... Supported Metallica (Marley Park), Black Sabbath, 9 Inch Nails, Motley Crew (Gods of Metal, Milan), European Tour with Thunder, Ash, Blue Oyster Cult, Wishbone, Thin Lizzy, The Who, & many many more.....
